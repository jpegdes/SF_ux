The redesign focussed on the research of the Sankurathri Foundation website, information architecture, developing a style guide and producing a hi-fi prototype with an objective to make it easier for visitors to easily scroll to the entire website content related Sankurathri Foundation activities and can donate easliy through the portal. Hence, we have great opportunity to use our design strategically to position ourself that properly convey SF mission.
- Design is not as per global standards.

- Language compatibility
- Engage users with the goal of increase awareness about the Sankurathri Foundation

- Design strategically that convey Sankurathri Foundation Mission

- Incorporate User Friendly interactive elements

- Enhanced design of SF home page and donation flow
Sankurathri Foundation is located in a serene locality with 5 acres of rural land, 10 km away from Kakinada was established by Dr. Chandrasekhar Sankurathri as a fond memorial to his wife Manjari Sankurathri, son Srikiran and daughter Sarada who were victims of the terrorist bombing of Air India Flight 182 (Kanishka) on 23rd June 1985.

Definition & Ideation
We began our initial research with redlining and a heuristic analysis. In analyzing the site, we were able to identify our proto-personas, Mrs. Maya and Sri. Rajesh. We then conducted one-on-one interviews with donaters ages 29-45 and deployed an anonymous survey to a group of teenagers. We input their notes into chits and organized them into an Affinity Diagram.
